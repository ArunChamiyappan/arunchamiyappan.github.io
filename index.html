About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. My research focuses on numerical modeling and simulation of additive manufacturing processes, with a particular interest in phase-field methods and finite element analysis. I am passionate about developing high-performance computing solutions using GPU acceleration to solve complex problems in materials science. My work aims to bridge the gap between computational modeling and real-world manufacturing applications.
